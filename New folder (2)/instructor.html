Mohammad Shidujaman

Mohammad Shidujaman received his Ph.D. degree in Design from Tsinghua University. His doctoral research emphasized developing expressive interaction design techniques for social robots that involve multidisciplinary research in Design for human-robot perceptual interconnection. His research interests include AI, Robotics, Interaction design, IoT, Computational Intelligence, Social and Cultural Anthropology, etc.
